# Customer Churn Analysis (Power BI)

## 📊 Overview
This project analyzes customer churn behavior using a telecom dataset to identify key factors driving customer attrition. The goal is to uncover actionable insights that can help improve customer retention and reduce churn.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Customer churn is a major challenge for subscription-based businesses. Understanding why customers leave allows companies to take proactive steps to improve retention and revenue.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI (Data Visualization)
- DAX (Measures & Calculations)
- Data Cleaning & Transformation

---

## 📈 Key Metrics
- Churn Rate (% of customers who left)
- Number of Churned Customers
- Customer Segmentation (Contract, Charges, Tenure, Services)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rates compared to long-term contracts
- Customers with higher monthly charges are more likely to churn
- Fiber optic users show higher churn behavior
- New customers (low tenure) are more likely to churn than long-term customers

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Encourage long-term contracts through incentives or discounts
- Offer targeted promotions to high-risk (high-charge) customers
- Improve onboarding experience for new customers
- Investigate service quality or pricing for fiber optic users
